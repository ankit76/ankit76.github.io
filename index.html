About Me

I am currently in my third year of study at IIT Bombay. I completed my higher secondary studies from S.P.College, Pune and secondary studies from Kawathekar Highschool, Pandharpur, my hometown. I would like to pursue a career in physics research.

Physics Interests

My interests are inclined toward theoretical physics. What I like about the subject, besides it describing the nature, is how elegantly connected everything is. Concepts appearing vastly different at the outset turn out to be closely related.

Through the experience with the nonlinear dynamics project, I'v developed a keen interest in computational physics. I was able to get a glimpse of the field of Condensed Matter Physics, while studying superconductivity and BECs on atomic chips. I found it fascinating to learn about the macroscopic manifestations of quantum mechanics. The study in this field entails use of concepts from a variety of different areas of physics and it also has many direct applications. I would also like to explore the field of quantum computation, although currently I've very little knowledge in this area. I would be interested in studying particle and high energy physics as well, after completing the field theory course.
